# Three Pillars Property Management Website

## 1. Project Description
A production-ready marketing website for Three Pillars Property Management, a Maryland-based property management company founded by a former NSA/Fort Meade employee. Target users: military/government employees on PCS or TDY who own property, and general Maryland property investors seeking transparent, personalized management. Core value: flat-fee transparent pricing ($175/mo or 8%, whichever is less), owner portal access, and personalized small-company service.

## 2. Page Structure
- `/` - Homepage (hero, trust bar, differentiators, pricing, services overview, military section, owner portal callout, testimonials, CTA)
- `/about` - About Us (founder story, mission, values, team)
- `/services` - Services (full-service management, tenant placement, marketing, maintenance, financial reporting, lease enforcement + pricing details)
